About Me

Welcome to my personal website. I am a graduate student in Sustainable Resource Management at the Technical University of Munich and a visiting student at the University of California, Davis. My academic background is in Geography, which I studied at Freie Universität Berlin.

My work focuses on environmental data analysis, climate-related research, and the use of computational and geospatial methods to better understand environmental change.

Background

I studied Geography at Freie Universität Berlin and am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Sustainable Resource Management at the Technical University of Munich.

Through my studies and research, I have developed a strong interest in environmental systems, sustainability challenges, and interdisciplinary approaches that combine scientific data analysis with geospatial methods.
