About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Sustainable Resource Management at the Technical University of Munich and am a visiting student at the University of California, Davis. My work focuses on environmental data analysis and geospatial approaches to studying environmental change.

I studied Geography at Freie Universität Berlin, where I developed an interest in environmental systems, spatial analysis, and data-based approaches to environmental research.

Research & Projects

I am currently supporting the Global Environmental Change Lab at UC Davis in the analysis of climate simulations using GDSTEM.

My broader interests include:

  • Climate and ecosystem research
  • Environmental data analysis using R and Python
  • Remote sensing and geospatial analysis using ArcGIS, QGIS, and eCognition
